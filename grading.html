Grading Breakdown

Grading for this class follows this rubric:

Type Percentage
Labs 50%
  • Lab 0: 5%
  • Lab 1: 7%
  • Lab 2: 8%
  • Lab 3: 12%
  • Lab 4: 18%
Midterm Exam 15%
Final Exam 25%
Participation 10%

Grading Details

Grade cutoffs are standard. 90-100 is the A range, 80-89 is the B range, and so on.

For students taking the class pass/fail, a passing grade will be equivalent to a D or better.

Participation is separated out to broaden the evaluation criteria, reduce the weight of the exams, and encourage participation in-class.
